About

My name is Peter Maxwell Thompson, I am a qualified web and print designer and illustrator.

I enjoy Adobe Illustrator above all else, using it to its full potential to make the majority of my designs. When editing complex visual designs, photographs or drawing designs Photoshop is my go-to program, which I have been using since 2010. While not used often for me I'm familiar with Adobe InDesign, Dreamweaver and Animation (Flash).

Also proficient at DSLR photography and general knowledge writing in Python, HTML, CSS and C# with the ability to step up to a job requiring more in-depth knowledge of a known language or learning a new one.
